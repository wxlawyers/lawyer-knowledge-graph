# Burden of Proof Rules — Detailed Reference

> Source: 2026-06-04 session, verified with 北大法宝

---

## Core Principle

**谁主张谁举证** (Whoever claims, bears the burden of proof)

Legal basis: 《民事诉讼法》第67条

---

## Application by Case Type

### General Rule (Most Cases)

| Claim | Burden of Proof | Who Applies for Appraisal |
|-------|-----------------|---------------------------|
| Product has quality defect | **Plaintiff** (buyer) | **Plaintiff** |
| Defendant breached contract | **Plaintiff** | **Plaintiff** |
| Loss amount | **Plaintiff** | **Plaintiff** |
| Plaintiff has fault (offset) | **Defendant** | **Defendant** |

### Exceptions (Burden Reversal)

| Case Type | Burden Reversed To | Legal Basis |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Product liability (tort) | **Producer** must prove no defect | 《产品质量法》第41条 |
| Environmental pollution | **Polluter** must prove no causation | 《民法典》第1230条 |
| High-risk operations | **Operator** must prove due care | 《民法典》第1236条 |
| Medical malpractice | **Hospital** must prove no fault (certain cases) | 《民法典》第1222条 |

**Important**: Product liability tort cases use burden reversal, but **contract quality disputes do NOT** — still follows general rule.

---

## Defendant's Appraisal Strategy

### When Defendant SHOULD Apply for Appraisal

1. **Defendant claims plaintiff has fault** (过错相抵) — defendant bears burden for this claim
2. **Defendant claims equipment meets standards** — may strategically apply
3. **Pre-empting plaintiff** — if plaintiff will likely apply, defendant may want to control the process

### When Defendant Should NOT Apply for Appraisal

1. **Denying quality defect exists** — burden is on plaintiff
2. **Default strategy** — let plaintiff bear the cost and risk
3. **When plaintiff hasn't provided evidence** — plaintiff should apply first

### Correct Court Statement

> "审判长，我方不认可设备存在质量问题。根据谁主张谁举证的原则，原告主张设备有质量缺陷，应当由原告举证证明。原告仅提供照片和自行委托的维修记录，未能证明设备变形系制造缺陷导致，应承担举证不能的法律后果。"

---

## Key Lesson from 2026-06-04

**Error**: Advised defendant to apply for appraisal in product quality dispute.

**Correction**: In contract quality disputes, burden is on plaintiff. Defendant should:
1. Deny quality defect exists
2. Demand plaintiff provide appraisal report
3. If plaintiff applies, participate but don't initiate
4. Only apply if claiming plaintiff has fault (过错相抵)

**Rule**: Don't make absolute statements ("defendant should NEVER apply"). Consider case type, claims, and strategy.
